# LexMastery Engineering Operating System (LEOS)

## Purpose

Welcome to the LexMastery Mobile Application.

This repository is not a standard Flutter project.

It is an AI-first engineering project.

Every AI agent working inside this repository must follow the Engineering Operating System (LEOS).

Do not begin implementing features until these rules have been read.

---

# About LexMastery

LexMastery is an AI-powered legal education platform that helps aspiring lawyers prepare for professional legal examinations such as:

- SQE
- NextGen Bar
- Future international legal qualifications

The platform combines

- Artificial Intelligence
- Cognitive Science
- Adaptive Learning
- Performance Analytics
- Legal Knowledge
- Accessibility

to become the world's most intelligent legal education ecosystem.

The product should feel like

"A personal AI legal mentor"

—not—

"an online course platform."
