# LexMastery Mobile

> AI-Powered Legal Education Platform

## Overview

LexMastery is an AI-first legal education platform designed to help law students and professionals prepare for:

- Solicitors Qualifying Examination (SQE)
- Nigerian Bar Examination
- Other jurisdiction-specific legal examinations

The platform provides:

- AI Tutor
- AI Instructor
- Structured Courses
- Mock Exams
- Flashcards
- Diagnostics
- Comparative Law
- Study Planner
- Progress Tracking
